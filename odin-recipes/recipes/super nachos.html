Super Nachos

Super Nachos are an irresistible and indulgent snack or meal, featuring a colorful and flavorful combination of tortilla chips piled high with a variety of delicious toppings. These nachos typically start with a base of crispy tortilla chips, which are then layered with seasoned ground meat (such as beef or chicken), melted cheese, and an array of toppings that can include diced tomatoes, sliced jalapeños, black olives, diced onions, and chopped cilantro. Additional toppings like sour cream, guacamole, salsa, and refried beans can be added to enhance the flavor and texture. Super Nachos are perfect for sharing with friends at parties or enjoying as a satisfying meal on their own. They offer a delightful blend of crunchy, cheesy, savory, and spicy flavors that make them a favorite comfort food for many.

Ingredients

  • 1 Large Bag Tortilla chips
  • 1 Pound Ground Meat (Beef, Chicken, Turkey)
  • 2 Cups Shredded Cheese (Cheddar, Monterey Jack, Mexican Blend)
  • 2 Medium Diced Tomatoes
  • 1/2 Cup Sliced jalapeños
  • 1/2 Cup Black olives, sliced
  • 1/2 Cup Diced onions
  • 1/4 Cup Chopped cilantro
  • 1/2 Cup Sour cream
  • 1/2 Cup Guacamole
  • 1 Cup Salsa
  • 1 Can Refried Beans (optional)
  • Lime Wedges (optional)
  • Additional toppings of your choice
  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the Ground Meat: In a skillet over medium heat, cook the ground meat until it's browned and fully cooked. Break it apart with a spoon as it cooks. Drain any excess fat from the skillet.
  3. Prepare the Toppings: While the meat is cooking, prepare the toppings. Dice the tomatoes, slice the jalapeños and black olives, chop the onions, and chop the cilantro. Prepare any additional toppings you'd like to include.
  4. Assemble the Nachos: Spread a single layer of tortilla chips on a large baking sheet or oven-safe dish. Sprinkle a layer of cooked ground meat evenly over the chips. Next, sprinkle a generous amount of shredded cheese over the meat.
  5. Add Toppings: Sprinkle diced tomatoes, sliced jalapeños, black olives, diced onions, and chopped cilantro evenly over the cheese.
  6. Optional Layer of Refried Beans: If desired, spread a layer of refried beans over the toppings.
  7. Bake: Place the baking sheet or dish in the preheated oven and bake for about 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Serve: Once the nachos are done baking, remove them from the oven and let them cool slightly. Serve the nachos warm with dollops of sour cream, guacamole, salsa, and any additional toppings of your choice.
  9. Garnish and Enjoy: Garnish the nachos with chopped cilantro and serve with lime wedges on the side for squeezing over the top if desired. Enjoy your delicious Super Nachos as a snack or meal!
